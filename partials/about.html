About

Tiny.House came about from a desire to provide accurate education to the community while maintaining the tiny philosophy. There are numerous websites out there both big and small that attempt to do this today, but I find the commercialism, and the willingness of these folks to sell you something, un-tiny. Nothing feels farther from the movement then a website with fourteen advertisements down the side, products that they where compensated to review, or trying to sell you something freely available. Being actively involved in my local tiny house community I often hear about different things people have paid for from an educational standpoint, and the lackluster quality of the materials they receive. Often this information is available for free around the internet.

Based on these frustrations, I decided the best thing to do would be to create a single place for people to educated themself. I also wanted to maintain the philosophy that I use with my tiny house community group. There is no owner to this website, it belongs to everyone. The code for this website is freely available, anyone can make modifications. All content is protected by the CC BY-NC-SA license and all software is protected by the AGPL license.

I look forward to seeing what we can build together as a community. A special thanks to the people below, as I couldn't have done it without them.

-TinyJ
