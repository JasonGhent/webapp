Community

Community is the single most important issue facing tine house enthusiasts today. When people band together people can more easily learn not only about tiny houses but work together to create the change that we need in todays climate to enable the lifestyle many of us desire.

This page has two sections, the first seciton is a list of groups around the world to help you find communtiy wherever you are. The second section contains hints and tips on starting your own community group.
